Yousef Hamza

Founder & CEO, V7 Capital | Visionary Investor | Mentor

Yousef Hamza is a prominent figure in the MENA region's business and investment sphere, blending legal acumen with entrepreneurial insight. A Kuwaiti national with a law degree from Cardiff University, Yousef has been instrumental in shaping the success of numerous start-ups and SMEs across the region.

As Founder and CEO of V7 Capital, Yousef has provided strategic, legal, and operational guidance to over 3,500 entrepreneurs, meeting with more than 50 SMEs each month to craft bespoke solutions for their unique challenges. Under his leadership, V7 Capital has grown into a trusted partner for businesses seeking to scale and thrive in a competitive environment.

Beyond V7 Capital, Yousef holds board positions and shares in companies such as Oliv, Merit Incentives, and School Voice. He also manages capital funds and collaborates with family offices, driving sustainable growth and innovation.

Prior to founding V7 Capital, Yousef served as Managing Partner at Envestors MENA, where he played a pivotal role in launching the region's first angel investment network. His efforts facilitated crucial connections between investors and entrepreneurs, significantly contributing to the growth of MENA's start-up ecosystem.

Yousef is actively involved in leadership initiatives, including Young Arab Leaders and the World Economic Forum’s Global Shapers Community, where he contributes to social and economic development. His work with the MENA Angel Investor Network further promotes early-stage investments that foster innovation and entrepreneurship.

A passionate mentor and educator, Yousef collaborates with organizations such as Seedstartup, Injaz, and Dubai+Acumen to support the next generation of business leaders. He is a sought-after speaker at universities and forums, where he shares his insights on entrepreneurship, fundraising, and strategic business growth.

Through his work, Yousef Hamza continues to be a driving force in the development of MENA's entrepreneurial ecosystem, shaping the future of business and investment in the region.
